PlayStation 4 Catalog

PS4 releases accelerate late in the console cycle

This local smoke test counts games by release year and focuses on the main 2013-2021 lifecycle. A few earlier and later rows exist in the source file, but they are sparse enough to read as edge-case records rather than the core catalog trend.

Read without hover: the visible pattern is a steady climb from 56 listed releases in 2013 to 1,793 in 2021.
Source: Yandex bootcamp dataset, playstation_4_games.csv.
